Company funding options

There are three primary ways companies finance their operations and growth in the short term and the long term: profits, debt financing, and equity financing. Profits are generated internally by the company, but debt and equity are external and are controlled by management decision making. Both debt and equity financing supply a company with capital, but the similarities largely stop there
